# VALLEY X Overview

## What is VALLEY X?
VALLEY X is a Phoenix-based community ecosystem built around digital rewards, local business engagement, and culture-driven participation.

It combines:
- Community activity
- Local business support
- Reward-based engagement
- Crypto-native identity

---

## Core Idea
Instead of traditional loyalty apps or coupons, VALLEY X introduces a unified reward system where users earn points through real engagement and spend them at participating businesses.

---

## Key Components

### 1. Community Layer
Telegram + X community where users engage, participate, and grow the ecosystem.

### 2. Rewards Layer
Users earn Valley Rewards Points through:
- Purchases at partner businesses
- Community engagement
- Events and missions

### 3. Merchant Layer
Local businesses integrate into the ecosystem by offering rewards, discounts, and perks.

---

## Goal
To build a local-first digital economy layer that connects culture, commerce, and community through engagement-based rewards.
