# VALLEY X Merchant Guide

## Overview
VALLEY X is a local-first rewards ecosystem that helps businesses attract new customers, increase repeat visits, and build stronger community engagement through a simple digital rewards system.

This guide explains how merchants can participate in the VALLEY X network.

---

## What VALLEY X Does for Your Business

VALLEY X helps local businesses by:

- Driving new customer traffic
- Increasing repeat visits
- Creating digital loyalty without complex apps
- Providing free community-driven marketing
- Connecting your business to local culture and events

---

## How It Works (Simple Version)

1. A customer visits your business  
2. They participate in the VALLEY X rewards system  
3. You confirm their purchase or engagement  
4. They receive Valley Rewards Points  
5. They return later to redeem rewards or perks  

---

## Merchant Benefits

### 1. More Customers
VALLEY X promotes participating businesses through:

- Local campaigns

---

### 2. Repeat Business
Customers are incentivized to return using rewards and redeemable perks.

---

### 3. Free Marketing Exposure
Participating businesses may be featured in:

---

### 4. No Complex Setup
No need for expensive software or apps. The system is designed to be simple and flexible for small businesses.

---

## Reward System (Merchant Side)

Merchants can choose how rewards are issued:

- Percentage-based rewards (example: 5%–15% back in points)
- Flat rewards per visit or purchase
- Special promotions or limited-time offers

Example:
- Customer spends $20
- Merchant issues reward (ex: 1,000–2,000 Valley Points)

---

## Redemption Options

Customers can redeem rewards for:

- Discounts on purchases
- Free items or upgrades
- Exclusive offers
- Event access or perks

Each merchant decides their own redemption rules within VALLEY X guidelines.

- Transactions may require verification (QR code or manual confirmation)
